CUBE

Architectural Association 2019-20. London, United kingdom

Description:

In order to achieve making a cube, i look at using only the corners, taking inspiration from the geometric shape within a cube, combined with the aesthetic combination of pyramids and cubes. The cube is made of 16 identical triangular steel sheets, bent and spot welded together.
